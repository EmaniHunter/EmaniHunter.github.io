Google Keep App - Redesign.

A tweaked design for my favorite organizational app!

To further my interest in UI design/engineering, I decided to redesign the most commonly used features of my favorite app as well as add new features. The entire process- wireframing, designing and prototyping was manifested with Figma.

Wireframes

The first idea that ran through my head was to add a new feature to the app that I felt would really improve my personal user experience

Feature added: Folders, to easily sort through notes. This feature replaces the "label" section of the current application. I then went on to briefly mock-up the other features I use most frequently.

Design

After putting together my wireframes, I decided it was time to fully manifest the new design.

Prototyping
